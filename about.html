ReverseLab

Hello! We are Reverselab; group of people who interested in security in South Korea.

Our goal is to achieve various practical security skills including.

  • Windows/Linux/Android System
  • Web Hacking
  • Digital forensic
  • Other knowledges about security

Studies

Main interest of ReverseLab Member is Capture The Flag(CTF).

CTF problems are one of good sources to train skills and think problems as multiple perspectives.

Also, Honor from wining awards is an additional benefit of CTFs.

But it is not enough to experience security siturations in wild.

So, we also try to find vulnerabilities in real-world softwares or embedded devices.

These studies match the goal of ReverseLab, and can be the background for each members to do significant activities in security world.

Team activity

ReverseLab is not exist for only security knowledges.

Team-works and relationship between members are important values for ReverseLab.

We get the "Team Meeting" periodically for making intimacy and sharing knowledges.

Besides, we get the team seminar or trips as team event.
